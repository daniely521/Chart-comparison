This website explores different types of charts and their uses.

Click on a chart type in the navigation to learn more.

Bar Chart

Uses:

  • Comparing values across different categories
  • Showing trends and patterns

Advantages:

  • Easy to read and understand
  • Visually effective for comparing discrete values

Disadvantages:

  • Can become cluttered with many categories
  • Difficult to compare subtle differences

Line Chart

Uses:

  • Showing trends and changes over time
  • Visualizing continuous data

Advantages:

  • Effective for visualizing trends and patters
  • Easy to see changes over time

Disadvantages:

  • Can be difficult to compare values at specific points
  • May not be suitable for large datasets

Pie Chart

Uses:

  • Showing the proportion of parts to a whole
  • Comparing the relative sizes of different categories

Advantages:

  • Easy to understand and interpret
  • Visually appealing for showing proportions

Disadvantages:

  • Difficult to compare small differences
  • Can be difficult to read with many categories
